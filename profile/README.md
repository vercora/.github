# Vercora

### Outcome markets, built as protocol infrastructure

Programmable markets for real-world and digital outcomes — **accessible**, **extensible**, and **designed for credible settlement**.

**Vercora Protocol** is a global layer for creating and operating **outcome-based markets**: not a single app or odds format, but infrastructure for creators, integrators, and communities who want clear rules and transparent resolution.

We’re **outcome-native** (resolution and market expression first), **protocol-first** (meant to be embedded and integrated), and **flexible by design** so pooled, matched, complete-set, AMM, and future models can share one stack. Participation is **open** where the protocol allows it; trust comes from **credible settlement** — mechanics you can verify, not a black box.

The reference deployment runs on [**Solana**](https://solana.com): sub-second finality and low fees keep markets responsive. **Live on devnet today; mainnet is next.**
